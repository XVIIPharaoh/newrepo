Making a Differnece in the World

Computers has helped the world progress at an accelerated pace and because of that it has improved the life quality of people around the world. To continue to improve quality of living we need to computer scientists to continue to create such machines. Here are some of the ways computer scientists has made a difference in the world.


Healthcare

There are so many aspects to where the healthcare industry benefited from computer science let’s start with keeping track of records. The hospitals use a database created by computer scientists to keep track of patients and their medical records. Another is the machines used by the staff of the like machine used to check blood pressure, defibrillators, cat scans, heartbeat monitor, and much more. Computer Scientist has made a huge impact in the healthcare industry and continue to farther influence as time progress on.

Schools

Before there were computers the only way to learn was to physically go to a school and listen to a teacher give a lesson, but now in today’s society we have computers and can listen to a lesson given by the teacher from anywhere. Also, schools use databases to keep track of students, faculty members, grades, attendance, and so on.

Environment

The environment is always a hot topic when it comes to pollution and deforestation. Here's how the technology Computer Scientist make helps with the deforestation issue. Before the best way to keep track of information was to write it down on paper which a lot of people was doing. This led to an increase in demand and lead to an absurd amount of tress getting cut down to please the demands. Now we have another way to store data and it doesn't involve in cutting down trees. When now store information on our computers that were made by Computer Scientist to make data storing a little greener.

Communication

Quick communication is a big advancement today. In earlier times communicating with someone in a different state would take weeks if not months depending on how far the distance. Also, news was slow to travel back in the day so getting important information to the public on the other side of the country. Fast forward to now where you can get a hold of someone on the other side of the country in a few moments is a significant difference. This is possibly because of the technology Computer Scientist made to better communications.
